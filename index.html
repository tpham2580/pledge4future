A New Frontier In Houston Organizing


Houston Youth Climate Strike and the Sunrise Movement have built hundreds of hubs and recruited thousands of organizers across the country. It has been no different in Houston, hundreds of organizers have hosted town halls, protested in the streets, and helped elect transformational candidates up and down the ballot.

From the Inner Loop to the Suburbs, from the Gulf in the East to the Bayous of the West, we are building an unprecedented movement of empowered young people that will not only shape the course of this election, but will define the next decade of politics in the Greater Houston Area. If we are to fight for racial, environmental, and economic justice, as H-town’s youth we must not only project our voices, but work methodically to gain power and influence in civic life. We must unite in an unparalleled, inclusive manner that forcefully communicates a vision for a future we know is possible.

All of that starts with this election. On the Trump administration’s watch, 75 billion-dollar weather and climate disasters have struck, over 1,000 black people have been killed by police, and over 200,000 people have died from COVID-19. We cannot let this continue.

Maybe it’s your first election. Maybe you still can’t vote. Maybe you’re nervous. That’s OK. Take a leap of faith and sign this pledge. By signing this pledge:

  1. I pledge to vote (if I am eligible). Check out the Sunrise Voting Guide if you’re unsure of who to support.
  2. I pledge to contact 3 other eligible voters I know by using Vote Tripling.
  3. If I can’t do (2), I will send this pledge to 3 other passionate young people to get them involved in this movement.

By providing us with your contact information, you will be joining one of the most powerful, diverse networks of young leaders ever assembled across Houston. Securing this election and defending our democracy will be the critical first step.
